Plottable Letters plays with letters, digits, and basic shapes. There is a great chance to obtain a "GM", but also many other letter combination.

Many NFTs are available to mint with an interesting variety of results. As in previous generators, owning the NFT confer the power to request a physical plot on: https://greweb.me/plots/nft – this is optional and you can also just enjoy the digital version.

As a generative plotter artist, I use code to create art (creative coding), draw with fountain pens on robots (plotting), and explore the boundaries of abstract art using algorithms in pursuit of increasingly realistic imagery. I do not produce prints; instead, I create unique 'plots' - physical works of art that are truly one-of-a-kind.
